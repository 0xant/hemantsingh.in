About

How I work, think, and make decisions

What I Do

I work on building and advising practical digital systems for Indian MSMEs. My focus is not on tools or trends, but on decisions that improve reliability, clarity, and long-term sustainability under real-world constraints.

My work spans operations, SaaS strategy, automation, and security-aware IT thinking — always grounded in real execution.

How I Think

  • Accuracy matters more than assumptions
  • Security is a design input, not an afterthought
  • Simple systems scale better than clever ones
  • Every abstraction has a cost
  • Operational clarity beats theoretical perfection

Background

I run and operate ventures across B2B operations, SaaS platforms, and consumer brands. This gives me direct exposure to supply chains, internal processes, customer behaviour, and system constraints.

I am not interested in generic frameworks. I prefer approaches that survive real-world friction, regulatory constraints, and imperfect users.

This has shaped a bias toward systems that remain understandable and operable even when conditions are imperfect.

What I Avoid

  • Over-engineering without operational need
  • Blind adoption of tools without understanding trade-offs
  • Security compromises for short-term convenience
  • Systems that only work in ideal conditions
